# Statistics & Fact-Based Content

Sleep science, Islamic statistics, research-backed insights.

## Template

```
[Statistic or fact]

[1 sentence context]

[Source: Study name, year, or "Scholars say" with qualifier]

[Relevance to Sunnah or SunnahSleep]
```

## Topics

- 7–9 hours sleep (CDC/WHO) — aligns with early Sunnah bedtime
- Right-side sleeping & acid reflux (clinical studies)
- Sleep deprivation & prayer focus
- Power nap benefits (20–30 min) — Qailulah
- Circadian rhythm & early sleep

## Fact-Check

**Required.** Every stat must have:
- Verifiable source (study, org, report)
- Link or citation
- No exaggeration or outdated data

## Red Flags

- "Studies show" without naming study
- Percentage without source
- Claiming causation where only correlation exists
